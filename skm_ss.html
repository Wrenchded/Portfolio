SKM Air Conditioning LLC - Internship Visuals

Here are detailed screenshots from my Business Analyst internship at SKM Air Conditioning LLC, showcasing the Power Apps maintenance scheduling application and related tools.

This is the main welcome and entry screen for the Preventive Maintenance Management System application, developed for SKM Air Conditioning Equipment. It signals the user's arrival at the system's interface, with a "Next" button to begin interaction.

This is the central dashboard of the Preventive Maintenance Management System, offering quick access to its six primary functionalities. Users can navigate to modules like scheduling, history, equipment, spare parts, and reporting from this main hub.

This interface, part of the "Schedule Maintenance" module, displays a list of machines on the left. When a machine is selected, the right panel populates with its specific details and a comprehensive checklist of maintenance tasks, including frequency and checks to be done, allowing for task assignment..

This is the "Maintenance Dashboard," providing an overview of all ongoing and scheduled maintenance tasks. It lists tasks with details such as Task ID, associated Machine ID, Assigned Date, Due Date, Progress status, Priority, Frequency, and the Technician assigned to each task.

This page lists all technicians and employees, showing their PF Number, Name, Designation, and Availability. This centralized view aids in managing and assigning tasks effectively

This page presents the "Machine Master" list within the PMMS, serving as a comprehensive inventory of all machines. It displays key details for each machine, including Machine ID, Name, Serial Number, Manufacturer, Model, Purchase Year, and Cost..
